Guess Reactivity Review

These are three visual directions for making the game feel more reactive. Each option includes a Guess button press treatment, a wrong guess reveal treatment, and a correct guess reveal treatment so you can choose a stronger interaction language before it goes into the app.

Option 1: Impact and Bloom

Wrong guesses hit hard with a red slam and recoil. Correct guesses feel uplifting with a soft green bloom. The button gets a confident kick and light sweep when pressed.
