Commanding Declamation: Whenever an enemy operative
would perform an action during an activation or counteraction
while visible to and within 6" of this operative, you can use this
rule. If you do, roll one D6: if the result is higher than that enemy
operative’s APL stat:
•  It cannot perform that action during that activation/
counteraction (the AP spent on it isn’t refunded). If it’s a
counteraction, that counteraction ends.
•  You cannot use this rule again during the battle.
