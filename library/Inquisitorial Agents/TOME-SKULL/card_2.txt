Expendable: This operative is ignored for your opponent’s kill/
elimination op (when it’s incapacitated, and when determining
your starting number of operatives). It’s also ignored for victory
conditions and scoring VPs if either require operatives to
‘escape’, ‘survive’ or be incapacitated by enemy operatives (if
it escapes/survives/is incapacitated, determining how many
operatives must escape/survive/be incapacitated, etc.).
Group Activation: Whenever this operative is expended, you
must then activate a ready friendly INQUISITORIAL AGENT
INTERROGATOR operative (if able) before your opponent
activates. The same is true in reverse (INTERROGATOR
followed by TOME-SKULL). When that other operative is
expended, your opponent then activates as normal.
