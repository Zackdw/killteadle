Boom!: If this operative is incapacitated during a battle in which
it hasn’t used its explosives, roll one D6, or two D6 if you wish. If

operative’s order, you cannot select Conceal. This operative

‘escape’, ‘survive’ or be incapacitated by enemy operatives (if
it escapes/survives/is incapacitated, determining how many
operatives must escape/survive/be incapacitated, etc.).
